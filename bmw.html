BMW, one of the world's most iconic luxury car brands, has a rich history spanning over a century. From its humble beginnings to its current status as a leader in innovation and performance, BMW has always been synonymous with excellence. Early Years (1916-1930s) Founded in 1916 as Rapp Motorenwerke, BMW initially produced aircraft engines before shifting to motorcycle production in 1923 and eventually cars in 1928. Post-War Era (1940s-1960s) After World War II, BMW faced significant challenges but persevered, introducing the iconic 501 model in 1952 and the groundbreaking 1500 "New Class" sedan in 1962. M3 and M5 (1980s-1990s)BMW has continued to push boundaries with innovative technologies like iDrive, EfficientDynamics, and electric/hybrid models like the i3 and X5 xDrive45e. Today and Beyond With a focus on sustainability, autonomous driving, and connectivity, BMW remains committed to delivering the ultimate driving experience, as seen in models like the 8 Series and M2 Competition. From its early days to its current status as a luxury car leader, BMW's passion for innovation and performance has driven the brand forward, making it a household name.
